Additional Frameworks

Deep-dive tools from specific chapters for targeted team-building challenges.

The Trust Equation for Global Teams

From Chapter 3. A modified trust formula that adds Cultural Intelligence as a key factor for distributed teams.

  • Trust = (Competence + Consistency + Connection + Cultural Intelligence) / Ego
  • Diagnose low trust by identifying which numerator is weak
  • Includes the Trust Repair Protocol for when trust breaks

The North Star Statement Builder

From Chapter 4. A structured process for co-creating a compelling team purpose statement.

  • Template: "We [transformation] for [who we serve] so that [impact]"
  • Three validation tests: Time Horizon, Decision Filter, Newcomer
  • Includes the Alignment Audit to verify purpose is real, not a slogan

The L-M-IC Partnership Assessment

From Chapter 1. Assess your team's L-M-IC maturity across four levels.

  • Level 1: Misaligned — Roles working in isolation or conflict
  • Level 2: Coordinating — Communicating but not truly partnering
  • Level 3: Collaborating — Actively partnering on key decisions
  • Level 4: Integrated — Fluid, self-reinforcing partnership

The Speak-Up Index

From Chapter 3. A seven-question anonymous survey measuring psychological safety. Designed for cultural sensitivity in global teams.

  • Seven research-backed questions rated 1–5
  • Scoring guide with action thresholds
  • Cultural interpretation guidelines
  • Recommended cadence: quarterly for established teams, monthly for new teams

The Conflict Style Matrix for Global Teams

From Chapter 3. Based on Erin Meyer's Culture Map, navigate different cultural approaches to conflict, feedback, and decision-making.

  • Three dimensions: Communicating, Evaluating, Leading
  • Cultural mapping guide for your team
  • Bridge-building strategies for each dimension
